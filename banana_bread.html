banana bread

prep time

15 mins

cook time

55 mins

Amount of servings

1 loaf

Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

step 2

Mash bananas; mix in butter, sugars, eggs, milk and vanilla.

step 3

Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in a separate bowl.

step 4

Mix dry ingredients into wet, and pour into pan,

step 5

Bake for 50-60 mins. Cool, slice and enjoy!

ingredients

1¾ cups of flour

½ tsp baking soda

dash salt

1 tsp cinnamon

½ tsp nutmeg

1 cup mashed bananas (3 bananas)

¾ cup white sugar

⅓ cup brown sugar

⅓ cup milk

2 eggs

1 tsp vanilla

½ cup melted butter

tools

Use over rippened bananas

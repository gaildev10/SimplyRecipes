BBQ Sauce

Homemade BBQ sauce

prep time

15 mins

cook time

0 mins

Tags : sauce

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a sauce pan over medium-high heat, saute onion and garlic for 2 minutes.

step 2

Add everythng else and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook uncovered, stirring occasionally for about 25 minutes. Sauce will thicken as it cools.

ingredients

2 tsp olive oil

½ cup onions (finely diced)

1 clove garlic (minced)

½ cup cider vinegar

½ cup water

1/2 cup ketchup

½ cup brown sugar

2 tbsp molasses

2 tbsp spicy mustard (Gulden's spicy brown)

1 tbsn worcestershire sauce

2 tsp chili powder

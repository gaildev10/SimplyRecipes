bbq spareribs

prep time

15 mins

cook time

2.5 hours

amount of servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

pre-heat oven to 300 F.

step 2

Rub ribs with lemon juice

step 3

Rub ribs with Rub mixture

step 4

Bake for 21/2 hours

step 5

Brush ribs with BBQ sauce and bake another 5 minutes

ingredients

¼ cups brown sugar

2 tsp chili powder

1 tsp salt

1 tsp dry mustard

1 tsp dried oregano leaves (not ground oregano)

½ tsp garlic powder

½ tsp onion powder

¼ teaspoon black pepper

1 rack of ribs

1 lemon (juiced)

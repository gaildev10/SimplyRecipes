Beef and Broccoli Noodles

A Dwayne favorite

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

amount of servings

6 servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl and mix it well.

step 2

Blanch the broccoli in the hot boiling water for 1-2 minutes, then remove from the water and set it aside in ice.

step 3

Heat the large wok/pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle 2/3 tbsp of oil and stir fry the minced beef until slightly brown.
Add 2 tbsp of stir-fry sauce mix over the beef.
Add garlic and onions.

step 4

Add the noodles and pour the sauce over them. The return the blanched broccoli to the wok.

step 5

Continue stir fry for another 1-2 minutes and finish with chili slices.

ingredients

250 g Minced/ground beef

400 g Noodles (fresh)/cooked egg noodles

200 g Broccoli

Half Onion

2 cloves garlic

2 red chili (optional)

2-3 tbsp cooking oil

For Stir Fry Sauce Mix:
2 tbsp Light Soy sauce

1 tbsp Dark Soy sauce

3 tbsp Oyster or vegetarian stir-fry

Sauce:
1 tbsp Brown Sugar

1 tbsp Shao Xing wine or Japanese mirin

1 tsp Sesame oil

beef stew

prep time

10 mins

cook time

15 mins

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Season meat.

step 2

Get skillet smoking hot and sear meat on all sides until you get a nice crust. Remove meat once color has developed.

step 3

Add in your onion and carrots. Cook until tender.

step 4

Add your tomato paste and beef base. Mix to combine.

step 5

Add your flour and cook for 1-2 minutes or until raw flour is cooked off.

step 6

Add beef stock and bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer.

step 6

Next, add in your Worcestershire sauce, fresh herbs and bay leaves.

step 7

Cover and cook on low for 1.5 - 2 hours or until Beef begins to get tender.

step 8

Next, add in your potatoes and celery for the final 20-30 minutes.

step 9

Season to taste.

step 10

Once the beef is fork tender and the veggies are tender, you can serve it. Serve in a bowl or over some white rice.

ingredients

2 lbs Beef Stew Meat (Chuck Roast)

1 lb small red potatoes

3-4 carrots

1 Yellow Onion

3-4 stalks of Celery

1 tbsp Garlic Paste

3 cups Beef Broth or stock

2 tbsps Tomato Paste

1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce

Fresh Rosemary and Thyme

1 tbsp Better than bouillon Beef Base

2 Bay Leaves

Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Onion Powder, Italian Seasoning, Cayenne

2-3 tbsps flour

1 cup frozen peas

Biscuits

Homemade buscuits

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

6 servings

Tags : breads

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.

step 2

Grate 1 stick of butter

step 3

Combine flour, baking powder, sugar and salt

step 3

Work in butter, followed by milk: Using a fork or 2 table knives, combine the butter and flour mixture until it resembles a coarse meal. Slowly add the milk, stirring with a fork, to the desired consistency.

step 4

Gently knead dough, then roll out: Turn the dough out onto a clean, lightly floured surface and gently knead just to bring the dough together. Carefully roll out the dough about 3/4 inch thick.

step 5

Cut into rounds and transfer to baking sheet: Using a biscuit cutter or sturdy glass, cut about 12 biscuits, re-rolling any scraps. Place on the lined baking sheet. Keeping them close to each other helps them rise.(The biscuits can be frozen at this point. Freeze on the baking sheet, then remove to a resealable container or plastic bag for easier storage.)

step 5

Lightly Brush biscuits with egg wash. Bake the biscuits for 13 to 15 minutes (add 3 to 4 additional minutes for frozen ones), until golden brown. Cool slightly and serve warm.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon salt

1 tsp sugar

1 stick unsalted butter

1 cup milk or buttermilk

1 egg (for egg wash)

tools

Cookie Cutter

Rolling Pin

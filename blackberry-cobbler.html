Blackberry Cobbler

Old-fashioned blackberry cobbler

prep time

15 mins

cook time

55-60 mins

amount of servings

8-10 servings

Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Preheat Oven: Preheat to 350°F (175°C).

step 2

Lightly grease a 9×13-inch baking dish.

step 3

Prepare the Berries:
Mix sugar and cornstarch. Toss blackberries with melted butter and cornstarch mixture, then spread in the dish.

step 4

Make the Batter:
Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Stir in milk, melted butter, and vanilla until combined but lumpy. Pour over berries.

step 5

Bake:
Bake for 55-60 minutes until the berries are tender and the crust is golden brown. Enjoy your delicious Blackberry Cobbler!

ingredients

½ cup white sugar

2 tbsp cornstarch

6 cups fresh blackberries

¼ cup unsalted butter, melted

Batter:
2½ cups all-purpose flour

1 ½ cups white sugar

1 tbsp baking powder

1 tsp salt

2 cups milk

¼ cup unsalted butter, melted

1 tbsp vanilla extract

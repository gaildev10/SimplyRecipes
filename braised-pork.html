Braised Pork

prep time

15 mins

cook time

2.5 hours

amount of servings

8-10 servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Preheat Oven: Preheat to 300Â°F.

step 2

Season pork with salt and pepper

step 3

Cut up vegetables into large chunks

step 4

Add oil to dutch oven pot to sear the pork. Add the pork (2-3 minutes per side). Remove pork and set aside.

step 5

Add the vegetables to the pot. Brown the veggies a bit then add half of the braising liquid (apple cider) to to scrap the bits from the pork.

step 6

Add the vegetables to the pot. Brown the veggies a bit then add half of the braising liquid (apple cider) to to scrap the bits from the pork.

step 7

Add tomato paste

step 8

Add the pork back into the pot

step 9

Add the rest of the braising liquid. Wait until it begins to simmer. Cover the pot

step 10

Place pot into the over (about 1.5 - 2 hours)

step 11

Pork should be "fall apart tender"...enjoy!

ingredients

Boneless Pork Roast

salt

black pepper

2 large white onions

3 carrots

3 celery

1 tbsp tomato paste

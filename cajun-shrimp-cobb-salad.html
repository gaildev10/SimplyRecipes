cajun shrimp cobb salad

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

Tags : salad

instructions

step 1

Chop lettuce. Add lettuce to bowl of iced water to keep crispy

step 2

chop onion, slice tomato, chop croutons

step 3

Season shrimp

step 4

Add oil to a pan, sear shrimp in frying pan

step 5

Assemble salad:
Lettuce, croutons, onions, tomatoes, eggs, shrimp, black pepper, shredded cheese

step 6

Add Vinaigrette

ingredients

Romaine lettuce

1 tomato

1/2 white onion

croutons

5-6 large shrimps

2 hard boiled eggs

shredded cheese

oil (to sear shrimp)

Shrimp Seasoning:
Cajun seasoning

Old bay seasoning

onion powder

garlic powder

black pepper

Apple Cider Vinaigrette Dressing:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

1/4 cup apple cider vinegar

1 tbsp honey

1 tsp Dijon Mustard

2 cloves garlic (minced)

1 tsp red crushed pepper

Black pepper

Pinch of salt

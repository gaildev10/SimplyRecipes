candied yams

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Peel and cut potatoes

step 2

In a dutch oven, add butter, add grangulated sugar and brown sugar.

step 3

Stir in 1 tablespoon of water and mix the sauce.

step 4

Add potatoes (medium/low heat)
coat all the potatoes with sauce

step 5

Add cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla and lemon juice (add a pinch of salt if using unsalted butter)

step 6

Cover pot half way
cook for 20 minutes on medium/low flame

ingredients

3 Large Sweet Potatoes, Peeled

½ Cup Granulated Sugar

1½ Cups Light Brown Sugar

½ Stick of Salted Butter

1 Tbsp Water

1 Tbsp Ground Cinnamon

¼ Tsp All Spice (or Nutmeg if you prefer)

1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

1 Tsp Lemon Juice

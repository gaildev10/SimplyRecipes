chicken and rigatoni in parmesan sauce

prep time

10 mins

cook time

25 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : italian Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Cook the Pasta:
Cook the rigatoni pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

step 2

Cook the Chicken:
In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook for 6-8 minutes until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

step 3

Prepare the Sauce Base:
In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of butter and minced garlic. Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Simmer the Broth: Pour in the chicken broth, stirring to combine, and bring it to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes to reduce slightly.

step 4

Make the Creamy Sauce:
Stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Mix until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes. Combine Ingredients: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, followed by the cooked rigatoni pasta. Toss everything together to coat the chicken and pasta in the sauce.

step 5

Serve and Garnish:
Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

ingredients

1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces

3 tablespoons butter

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup chicken broth

1 cup heavy cream

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

12 oz rigatoni pasta

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

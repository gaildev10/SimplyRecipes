Chicken Hash
(Chicken Potatoes Onions)

Make chicken and potatoes with just a few ingredients.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

25 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Slice a yellow onion

step 2

Dice garlic cloves.

step 3

Dice cilantro

step 4

Peel and cube potatoes

step 5

Cube chicken

step 6

Add oil to bottom of frying pan

step 7

Brown chicken

step 8

Once you turn chicken, add cumin, fennel, dried thyme, red pepper flakes and paprika. Stir.

step 9

Add onions. Stir until onions are softened

step 10

Remove chicken and onions

step 11

Add potatoes. Add a touch more oil. Add some salt and pepper. Add lid and cook until potatoes are softened.

step 12

When potatoes are soft...add garlic and cilantro.

step 13

Return the chicken and the onions. Stir

step 14

Lower the temperature and add a little lemon juice.

step 15

Garnish with cilantro and serve!

ingredients

2 Tbs vegetable oil

2 lb. Chicken thighs, skinless and boneless, cut into bite size

½ Tsp Cumin seeds

½ Tsp Fennel sedds

¼ Tsp Thyme, dry

1½ Tsp Paprika

Pinch red pepper flakes ( optional )

1 Yellow onion, ,peeled and sliced medium size

3 Yukon gold, peeled and cut into small bite size cubes

1 Tbs Garlic, chopped

¼ C Cilantro, chopped for cooking and garnishing

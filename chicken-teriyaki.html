chicken teriyaki

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings
Tags : chicken Tags : asian

instructions

step 1

Cup up bell peppers (add to baking sheet)

step 2

Dice onion

step 3

Cut up broccoli into bite sizes

step 4

Mince fresh giner

step 5

Cut up chicken thighs into bite sizes.

step 6

Add all to a sheet pan.

step 7

Make the sause:
Add soy sauce, pineapple juice, add honey, add vinegar, minced ginger, minced garlic to a pot.

step 8

Make the slurry:
2 tsp corn starch and 2 tsp water. Add to sauce. Mix in a pot over medium heat.

step 9

Glaze the chicken & vegetables with the sauce.

step 10

Add baking sheet to 400 degrees oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

step 11

Drizzle extra sauce over rice and chicken when serving.

ingredients

1½ pounds chicken thighs

2 cups broccoli florets

1 red bell pepper

1 red onion, diced

Teriyaki Sauce:
⅓ cup low sodium soy sauce

⅓ cup pineapple juice (orange juice or mango juice)

3 tablespoons honey or maple syrup

3 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (or rice vinegar)

1 tablespoon minced garlic

2 teaspoons grated ginger

tools

Serve with Rice

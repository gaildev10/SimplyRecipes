chili

Chili con carne or "chili with meat" - a spicy Mexican stew.

prep time

15 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

20 servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

In a large dutch oven or large pot, saute the diced bell pepper and onions in olive oil until they’re translucent and start to caramelize.

step 2

Add in your seasoning, minus the baking soda. Cook for one to two minutes while stirring continuously.

step 3

Add your ground meat and brown until it’s just done. It’s okay if it still has some pink in it before you add the rest of your ingredients because you’re going to continue cooking it.

step 4

Pour in your tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and water.

step 5

Bring to a simmer over medium-low heat and cook for about 15 minutes. Add the drained beans.

step 6

Stir in the baking soda, if desired. This will cause the sauce to start to bubble up. Keep stirring over medium-high heat until the bubbles have gone away, then reduce the heat.

step 7

Remove from heat and you’re ready to serve. You can use salt and black pepper to taste if you’d like.

ingredients

2 pounds ground beef

2 small green bell peppers, about 2 cups diced

small yellow onion, about 1½ cups diced

2 (15-ounce) cans diced tomatoes

15-ounce can tomato sauce

6-ounce can tomato paste

15 ounces of water

2 (15-ounce) cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed

3 tablespoons chili powder

2 tablespoons garlic powder

2 tablespoons onion powder

3 tablespoons cumin

1 tablespoon paprika

1 teaspoon chipotle powder

½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

½ tablespoon salt

1 teaspoon white pepper

½ teaspoon baking soda, optional to cut acidity

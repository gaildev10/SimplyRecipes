prep time

15 mins

cook time

50 mins

amount of servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Brown the ground beef

step 2

Chop and then add peppers and onions to the ground beef.

step 3

Add seasonings

step 4

Create a well in the pot and add tomato paste and mix.

step 5

Add a can of Lager

step 6

Add Tomato sauce and Crushed tomatoes.

step 7

Add Beef Broth, mix and let simmer for 45 minutes.

step 8

Add 2 cans of beans.

step 9

Cook for another 30 minutes.

ingredients

1 lb ground beef

green pepper

red pepper

1 large onion

nature seasoning

cumin

smoked paprika

cayanne pepper (just a touch)

cajun seasoning

1 tbsp tomato paste

1 can of lager

tomato sauce

crushed tomatoes

beef stock

2 can of beans

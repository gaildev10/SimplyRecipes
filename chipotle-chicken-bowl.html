chipotle chicken bowl

prep time

20 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Add the chicken to a bowl with the rest of the ingredients for the marinade. Mix until chicken is evenly coated and then cover and refrigerate for at least 20 minutes.

step 2

Wash the rice until the water runs clear, and then drain it with a fine mesh sieve. Transfer to a medium pot and add the water and salt. Bring to a boil over high heat, then cover and simmer on low heat for 15 mins. Remove the pot from the heat and let rest, covered for 10 mins, tnen stir in the cilantro, lime and butter.

step 3

In a small pot over medium heat, add the black beans, cumin, chili powder and salt. Mix and cook until warmed through (about 5 min).

step 4

Preheat a large pan over medium high heat. Add 1 tbsp oil and sear chicken 5-6 mins. per side (10-12 mins.total) until internal temp reaches 165F. Let rest 5 mins. then cut in small cubes.

step 5

Build your bowls with rice, beans, romaine and chicken, then top with your favorite toppings such as guacamole, salsa, cheese, and sour cream. Enjoy!

ingredients

Chicken & Marinade:
1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs

1 lime (juiced)

2 tbsp finely chopped fresh cilantro

1½ tsp chili powder

1½ tsp cumin

1 tsp kosher salt

¼ tsp pepper

2 tbsp olive oil (plus more for cooking the chicken)

Beans:
15 oz. can black beans (drained)

½ tsp cumin

¼ tsp chili powder

¼ tsp kosher salt

Rice:
1 cup long grain white rice (uncooked)

1½ cups water

½ tsp kosher salt

3 tbsp finely chopped fresh cilantro

1 lime (juiced)

1 tbsn butter

Bowls(options):
Romaine lettuce (sliced thin)

Shredded cheese

Salsa or salsa verde

Guacamole or Avocado

Sour cream

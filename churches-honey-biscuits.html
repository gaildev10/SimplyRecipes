churches honey biscuits

prep time

10 mins

cook time

25 mins

baking temp

375° F

Tags : breads Tags : breakfast

instructions

step 1

In a bowl add flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and shredded butter.

step 2

Combine the ingredients together (use hands) until the butter is mixed with the flour. (flour will become course)

step 3

Add milk and mix into rest of floured bowl.

step 4

Spray a baking pan with Pam.

step 5

Use an ice cream scooper to add the biscuits to the pan.

step 6

Bake for 25 minutes.

step 7

Mix together Honey and melted butter.

step 8

When Biscits are done, Add Honey/Butter to the tops of biscuits and serve.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tbsp baking powder

1 tbsp sugar

1 tsp salt

1 stick of butter

⅔ to ¾ cup milk

The Glaze:
½ cup butter (melted)

¼ cup honey

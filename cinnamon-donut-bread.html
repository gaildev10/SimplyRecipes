cinnamon donut bread

prep time

15 mins

cook time

50 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

step 2

Grease a 9×5-inch loaf pan with butter or nonstick spray.

step 3

Mix the Dry Ingredients:
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg (if using), and salt.

step 4

Mix the Wet Ingredients:
In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, melted butter, and vanilla extract until well combined.

step 5

Combine Wet & Dry Ingredients:
Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.

step 6

Do not overmix—this will keep the bread light and fluffy.

step 7

Pour and Add Topping:
Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.

step 8

Pour and Add Topping:
In a small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar and cinnamon for the topping.

step 9

Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the batter.

step 10

If desired, drizzle melted butter over the topping for extra crispiness.

step 11

Bake to Perfection:
Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

step 12

Let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

step 13

Slice and Enjoy!
Cut into thick, soft slices and serve warm.

step 14

Enjoy with butter, honey, or your favorite spread!

ingredients

For the Bread:
2 cups all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon baking soda

1½ teaspoons ground cinnamon

½ teaspoon salt

2 large eggs

1 cup buttermilk

½ cup unsalted butter, melted

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

½ teaspoon nutmeg (optional for extra warmth)

For the Sugar Topping:
¼ cup granulated sugar

2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

1 tablespoon melted butter (optional for extra crispiness)

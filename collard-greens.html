Collard Greens

Slowly simmered with smoked ham.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

2hours 30mins

amount of servings

8-10 servings

Tags : vegetables

instructions

step 1

Place hocks in Dutch oven or large saucepan; add water, pepper pods and garlic. Cover. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 2 hours or until hocks are tender.

step 2

Break off stems of greens. Wash leaves throughly. Slice leave into bite-size pieces by rolling several leaves together and slicing in 1/4-inch strips.

step 3

Add greens, sugar and salt to hocks. Cook 30-40 minutes or until greens are done.

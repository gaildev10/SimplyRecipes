Cornbread

soft, moist and buttery

prep time

5 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

8 servings

Tags : bread

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.

step 2

In a bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt. In another bowl, combine the eggs, buttermilk, melted butter and sugar. Stir the egg mixture into the flour mixture.

step 3

Grease a 9-inch cast-iron skillet or a 9 x 13-inch baking pan with butter and dust it with a little cornmeal. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30 minutes, until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let the cornbread cool a bit before slicing and serving.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1⅓ cups yellow cornbread, plus extra for dusting the pan

4¼ teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon salt

3 eggs

1⅔ cups buttermilk

⅔ cup melted unsalted butter, plus butter for greasing the pan

½ cup sugar

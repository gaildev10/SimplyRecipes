Crispy Fried Chicken Sandwich

Delicious crispy fried chicken

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Make marinade:
Stir together 1/2 cup picklebrine, 1 tablespoon salt, paprika, 1 teaspoon pepper, cayenne pepper, garlic powder & onion powder.
place mixture in a plastic bag along with the chicken. (Marinate for 2 or more hours)

step 2

Create wet batter: Buttermilk, 1/2 cup water, egg
Add chicken

Mix Dry batter: Flour, cornstarch, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper

step 3

Dip wet chicken into flour

step 4

Add floured chicken to hot oil (360 degrees)

ingredients

½ cup pickle brine

2 tablespoons kosher salt (divided)

2 teaspoons paprika

1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon black pepper (divided)

½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

½ teaspoon garlic powder

½ teaspoon onion powder

12 boneless, skinnless chicken thighs (about 3 pounds)

1 cup buttermilk

½ cup water

1 large egg

4 cups all-purpose flour

¼ cup cornstarch

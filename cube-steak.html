cube steak & gravy

Melt in your mouth Tender

prep time

15 mins

cook time

2 hours

amount of servings

3 servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

heat skillet with oil (Just enough to cover the bottom);
pre-heat oven to 325 F.

step 2

Season the steaks:
seasoning salt, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper

step 3

flour steaks with flour

step 4

(shake off excess flour first)
brown steaks:
Add steak to frying pan (stove temp to about 5 o'clock...not too high)
Don't over crowd skillet
cook just enough to brown...don't cook all the way. It will cook thoroughly in the oven.

step 5

Get rid of extra oil...just a little to make gravy. Keep all of the bits from the steak.

step 6

Use the same flour used to flour the meat...add about 3 tablespoons to the hot oil.

step 7

Stir the oil and flour...do not burn.

step 8

if flour is too dry/thick, add a little more of the discarded oil.

step 8

Cook and stir until flour looks brown...don't burn it

step 9

Once color is brown, add hot water (1 cup at a time); keep stirring. Add water until it looks like the amount of gravy that you want.

step 10

Season gravy:
Add salt, garlic powder, onion powder and pepper (season to taste). Keep stirring. (turn temp to about 5 o'clock).

step 11

Gravy is loose but will thicken in the oven.

step 12

Spray baking dish, then add cube steaks.

step 13

cover steaks with cut onion.

step 14

Pour gravy over the steak.

step 15

Add pepper to top and cover baking dish with aluminum foil.

step 16

Leave in oven for 1.5 hours

ingredients

6 cube steaks

seasoning salt

onion powder

garic powder

1 onion

flour

water

black pepper

cooking oil

fried pork chops sandwich

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Add seasonings to pork chops

step 2

Add buttermilk

step 3

To another bowl add flour and cornstarch

step 4

Add wet pork chops to flour

step 5

Add pork chops to hot oil and fry to golden brown

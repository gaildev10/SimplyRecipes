garlic herb rice

Tags : rice

instructions

step 1

Add oil to coat bottom of frying pan

step 2

add rice

step 3

Toast rice in the hot oil (make sure every grain of rice gets oil)

step 4

Add 5 garlic cloves

step 5

Add oregano, marjoram, herbs de provence

step 6

Stir rice until golden brown (it can burn fast so be careful)

step 7

Add 2 cups water

step 8

Add chicken boullion

step 9

Stir rice. As soon as liquid cooks out cover with a lid and cook on low flame for 10 minutes

ingredients

1 cup rice

oil

5 garlic cloves

oregano

marjoram seasoning

herbs de provence

2 cups of water

chicken boullion

tools

Ham

A great Holiday recipe

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat and saute onion and garlic for 2 minutes.

step 2

Add everything else and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, for about 25 minutes.
Sauce will thicken as it cools.

step 3

Reserve 1 cup of Glaze mixture to add to the pot with the ham later.

step 4

Bring the remaining mixture to a boil then lower heat and simmer until reduced and thickened to a glaze (about 15-20 minutes)

step 5

Heat the oil in a Dutch oven (high heat).
Place the ham in the pot (flat side down) and cook until golden brown (about 2 minutes).
Using 2 forks, turn the ham and brown on all sides.

step 6

Arrange the ham (flat side down) and add the reserved glaze mixture to the pot.

step 7

Bake the ham in the oven until cooked thoroughly (about 1 hour), basting the ham with the remaining thickened glaze every 15 minutes.

ingredients

2 tsp olive oil

½ cup water

½ cup ketchup

½ cup brown sugar

2 tbsp molasses

2 tbsp spicy mustard (Gulden's spicy brown)

1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

2 tsp chili powder

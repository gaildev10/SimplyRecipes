Honey garlic butter ribs

Natural and Rich Spices

prep time

15 mins

cook time

70 mins

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Rinse the ribs thoroughly in running water

step 2

Remove the ribs membrane with a butter knife

step 3

Remove the moisture with paper towels and then slice the ribs into sections

step 4

Cover each rib with rub mixture

step 5

Place ribs on aluminum lined baking tray

step 5

Place ribs in preheated oven (350 degrees) for 50 minutes. Then turn the meat in the oven. Continue coking ribs for another 30 minutes.

step 6

In a frying pan, melt butter (4 tbsp); add 3 tbsp garlic.
Coat each rib with butter/garlic mixture.
add vinegar (stir); add honey (stir)
add cloves of garlic (stir)

step 7

Plate and serve

ingredients

Dry Rub:
4 tbsp sugar

4 tbsp paprika powder

4 tbsp cumin

2 tbsp salt

2 tbsp garlic powder

2 tbsp onion powder

2 tbsp black pepper

Honey Lemon Pepper Wings

prep time

15 mins

cook time

40 mins

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 450°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

step 2

Pat dry the chicken wings with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.

step 3

In a large bowl, combine potato starch, baking powder, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika. Toss the chicken wings in the mixture until evenly coated.

step 4

Place the coated wings on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer, ensuring they’re not touching.

step 5

Lightly spray the wings with oil to help them crisp up in the oven.

step 5

Bake the wings at 450°F for 35-40 minutes, flipping them halfway through to ensure even cooking.

step 6

While the wings are baking, prepare the Honey Lemon Pepper Sauce. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine honey, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, butter, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 3-5 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

step 7

Once the wings are cooked through and crispy, transfer them to a large bowl.

step 8

Pour the Honey Lemon Pepper Sauce over the wings and toss until evenly coated.

step 8

Serve hot and enjoy the deliciousness!

ingredients

2 pounds chicken wings

3 tablespoons potato starch

1 tablespoon baking powder

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon onion powder

¼ teaspoon paprika

Oil spray

For the Honey Lemon Pepper Sauce:
½ cup honey

Zest of 1 lemon

¼ cup lemon juice (or more to taste)

1 tablespoon minced garlic

1 tablespoon butter

1-2 teaspoons ground pepper

Pinch of salt

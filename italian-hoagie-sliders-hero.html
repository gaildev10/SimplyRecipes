italian hoagie sliders hero

prep time

5 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : sandwiches

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

step 3

Cut the slider rolls in half lengthwise so that you have a sheet of top and bottom rolls. Place the bottom sheet on the baking sheet.

step 3

Layer on the provolone cheese, ham and salami – be sure to put the cheese on the bottom so that when it melts you are still able to take off the top roll layer to add toppings.

step 4

Place the top roll layer on top of the salami. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle on garlic powder and sesame seeds. Bake at 350F for 8-10 minutes.

step 5

While the sliders bake, cut the tomato into thin slices. Add mayo, vinegar and Italian seasoning to a mixing bowl and whisk together. Add the shredded lettuce to the bowl and toss to combine.

step 6

When the sliders are finished baking, transfer to a cutting board. Take off the top roll layer and layer on the thinly sliced tomato and onion – season with salt. Then add the lettuce mixture and finally the banana peppers.

step 7

Place the top roll back and cut into individual sandwiches – serve immediately!

ingredients

1 sheet slider rolls 12 rolls in total – I use King’s Hawaiian brand

⅓ lb provolone cheese

⅓ lb deli sliced ham

⅓ lb deli sliced genoa salami

2 tbsp butter – melted

Garlic powder and sesame seeds for topping the rolls

1 large tomato

2 tbsp mayonnaise

cooking oil

½ tbsp red wine vinegar

1 tsp italian seasoning

½ a white onion – thinly sliced

2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce

1 cup sliced banana peppers or sliced hot cherry peppers, optional

jambalaya with chicken

prep time

15 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Brown andouille sausages on medium high temperatures (set aside)

step 2

In same skillet brown chicken thighs. Season with all purpose seasoning. Set aside.

step 3

Add to pan peppers, onions and celery (reduce to Medium temperature)

step 4

Add garlic paste, tomato paste and crushed tomatoes.

step 5

Mix in 1 1/2 cup rice; add the sausage and chicken back. Add chicken broth.

step 5

Season and let it all come to a boil.

step 6

Turn down heat and let cook for about 12 minutes. Turn everything off and let it fluff.

step 7

Garnish with green onions.

ingredients

1 lb chicken thighs (cubed)

13 oz Cajun andouille sausage

1½ cups med-long grain rice

2 celery stalks (diced)

1 green pepper (diced)

1 yellow onion (diced)

4 cloves garlic (minced) or garlic paste

1 tbsp tomato paste

½ cup crushed tomatoes or diced tomatoes

28 oz. Chicken broth

1 tbsp avocado oil

2 tbsp all purpose seasoning

2 tsp garlic and herb seasoning

2 tsp Cajun seasoning

Green onions for garnish

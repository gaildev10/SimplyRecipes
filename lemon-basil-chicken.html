lemon basil chicken

A delicious one pan chicken recipe that combines chicken breasts, garlic and basil in a rich creamy lemon sauce.

prep time

15 mins

cook time

25 mins

amount of servings

8 servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Place chicken pieces between sheets of plastic wrap and pound with a mallet evenly and gently until they are 1/4 inch thick.

step 2

Dredge each piece with salt and pepper.

step 3

In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon each butter and oil.

step 4

When butter is dmelted, add as many chicken pieces as will fit without crowding and cook quickly for a minute or two on each sides, or until the chicken losses its pinkness inside.

step 5

Remove chicken pieces to a hot platter and keep warm.
Cook rest of chicken pieces, adding more butter and oil as needed and remove to a warm platter. (May use 200 F oven.)

step 6

Add the wine and lemon juice to the pan and cook over medium heat, stirring to blend in browned particles and juices. Boil until reduced by about half.

step 7

Add the cream, Lemon basil, thyme and parsley. Boil until sauce thickens slightly. Pour any juice that colledcted on the chicken meat platter into the skillet.

step 8

Taste sause for seasoning, adding salt and pepper if needed.

step 9

Pour sauce over the sauteed chicken and garnish with Lemon basil leaves and lemon slices.

step 10

Serve over rice or buttered noodles

ingredients

4 skinned and boned chicken breasts

salt

freshly ground pepper

butter

olive oil

½ cup dry wine

1 tablespoons lemon juice

½ cup heavy cream

⅓ cup lemon basil

½ teaspoon fresh thyme leaves (or ¼ teaspoon dried)

2 teaspoons fresh minced parsley

lemon basil leaves

lemon slices

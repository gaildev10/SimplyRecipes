Macaroni and Cheese

Cheesy and Creamy side dish

prep time

15 mins

cook time

45 mins

amount of servings

Tags : pasta

instructions

step 1

In a dutch oven:
Add carnation evaporated milk

step 2

Add cheese (cut in cubes)

step 3

Boil water for pasta

step 4

After cheese has melted add seasonings
Add season Salt
Add a pinch of table salt (not too much because season salt is very salty)
Add black pepper
Add mustard
Add eggs

step 4

Add macaroni to boiling water (cook for 7 minutes...al dente)
Remove from stove

step 5

Add cooked pasta to cheese mixture

step 6

Add mac and cheese to a baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil

step 7

Bake mac & cheese in 350 degree temp oven for 40 minutes.
Remove aluminum foil and cook until golden brown

ingredients

1 can carnation evaporated milk

2 packs of chedder cheese (mild)

1 pack of chedder cheese (sharp)

season salt

salt

black pepper

2 eggs, lightly beaten

1 tbsp gulden's spicy mustard

1 box of elbow macaroni

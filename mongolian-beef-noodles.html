Mongolian Beef Noodles

Easy to make Ground Beef Mongolian Noodles...sweet and spicy.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

Tags : beef asian

instructions

step 1

1. Cook the Noodles: ◦ Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. ◦ Add the noodles and cook according to package instructions until al dente. ◦ Drain and set aside.

step 2

Prepare the Sauce: ◦ In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, beef broth, brown sugar, and crushed red pepper flakes (if using). ◦ Set the sauce aside.

step 3

Cook the Ground Beef: ◦ Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. ◦ Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. ◦ Drain any excess fat.

step 4

Add Aromatics: ◦ Reduce the heat to medium. ◦ Stir in the minced garlic and ginger, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.

step 5

Combine Sauce and Beef: ◦ Pour the prepared sauce into the skillet with the cooked beef. ◦ Stir well to combine, and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce slightly thickens.

step 6

Mix in the Noodles: ◦ Add the cooked noodles to the skillet, tossing to coat them evenly with the beef and sauce mixture. ◦ Cook for an additional 2 minutes to heat through.

step 7

Serve: ◦ Transfer to serving plates. ◦ Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Enjoy hot!

ingredients

Noodles: 8 oz (225g) linguine or spaghetti

Vegetable Oil: 2 tablespoons

Ground Beef: 1 lb (450g) lean

Garlic: 3 cloves, minced

Ginger: 1 teaspoon fresh, minced

Soy Sauce: ¼ cup (60ml) low-sodium

Hoisin Sauce: ¼ cup (60ml)

Beef Broth: ¼ cup (60ml)

Brown Sugar: 2 tablespoons

Crushed Red Pepper Flakes: ½ teaspoon (optional)

Green Onions: 2, sliced (for garnish)

Sesame Seeds: (for garnish)

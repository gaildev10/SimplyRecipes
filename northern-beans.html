great northern beans

prep time

10 mins

cook time

2 hours

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : legumes

instructions

step 1

Soak beans in water overnight.

step 2

In a Duth oven, Saute in olive oil onions, celery, garlic

step 3

Add neck bones & beans. Cover with water. Add seasonings.

step 4

Bring to a boil...then turn to low heat and cook for 2 hours.

ingredients

1 lb of great northern beans (soaked in water overnight)

Smoked meat (I used neck bones)

Water (enough to cover the smoked meat)

2 tablespoons of chicken bouillon

2 packets of beans seasoning

Onion powder

Garlic powder

black pepper

2 bay leaves

2 stalks celery

1 yellow onion

garlic cloves (about 4)

salt (very little)

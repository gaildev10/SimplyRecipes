Oatmeal raisin cookies

This simple orange-scented yellow cake.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

10 mins

amount of servings

36 cookies

Tags : cookies Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Using a hand mixer or a stand mixer fitted with paddle attachment, cream the softened butter and both sugars together on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add the eggs and mix on high until combined, about 1 minute. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl as needed. Add the vanilla and molasses and mix on high until combined. Set aside.

step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together. Add to the wet ingredients and mix on low until combined. Beat in the oats, raisins, and walnuts (if using) on low speed. Dough will be thick, yet very sticky. Chill the dough for 30-60 minutes in the refrigerator (do the full hour if you’re afraid of the cookies spreading too much). If chilling for longer (up to 2 days), allow to sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes before rolling and baking.

step 3

Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.

step 4

ARoll balls of dough (about 2 tablespoons of dough per cookie) and place 2 inches apart on the baking sheets. I recommend using a cookie scoop since the dough can be sticky. Bake for 12-14 minutes until lightly browned on the sides. The centers will look very soft and under-baked. Remove from the oven and let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. The cookies will continue to “set” on the baking sheet during this time.

step 5

Beat in orange zest and orange juice.

step 6

Stir in flour mixture until smooth.
Scrape the batter into the pan and spread evenly.
(if desired sprinkle 1 heaping tablespoon of sugar on top.)

step 7

Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. (about 30-35 minutes) Let cool in pan on a rack for 10 minutes. Slide a thin knife around the cake to detach it from the pan. Invert the cake. Let cool on the rack.

ingredients

1 cup (16 Tbsp; 226g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature

1 cup (200g) packed light or dark brown sugar

1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

2 large eggs*

1 Tablespoon pure vanilla extract (yes, Tablespoon!)

Tablespoon (15ml) unsulphured or dark molasses (do not use blackstrap; I prefer Grandma’s brand)

and 2/3 cups (209g) all-purpose flour (spooned & leveled)

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 and 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon salt

3 cups (255g) old-fashioned whole rolled oats*

1 cup (140g) raisins

oxtails

Tender and Delicious

prep time

15 mins

cook time

3 hours

amount of servings

0 serving

Tags : stews

instructions

step 1

Wash and trim excess fat off of the oxtails.

step 2

Add oxtails to a roasting dish.
Season them with garlic paste, sofrito, oregano, adobo, black pepper, soy sauce and oil. Add tomato paste and give a quick mix.

step 3

Add vegetables:
red pepper, green pepper, red onion and cilantro
combine into the oxtails.

step 4

Add hot sauce and cognac
give it a good mix then pour 1 cup of water over the oxtails.

step 5

Cover with aluminum, tightly give it a good mix then pour 1 cup of water over the oxtails.

step 6

Bake in a pre-heated oven at 325 degrees for 2.5 hours.

step 7

Remove foil and bake for another 3 minutes.

ingredients

3 lbs oxtails

2 tbsp garlic paste

2 tbsp sofrito

1 tbsp oregano

1 tbsp adobo

1 tbsp black pepper

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp oil (Achiote)

2 tbsp tomato paste

½ red pepper

½ cubanelle pepper (green pepper)

½ red onion

Bunch of fresh cilantro

Hot sauce to taste

2 oz cognac (or any dark liquor)

1-2 cups water

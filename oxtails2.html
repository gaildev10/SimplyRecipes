oxtails

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

amount of servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Remove excess fat from oxtail meat using a sharp knife and a cutting board. Rinse meat under cold running water. Drain and pat dry.

step 2

In a large bowl, season oxtails with powdered seasonings, chopped garlic and gravy master sauce.

step 3

In a blender, add 3 garlic cloves (whole), small whole tomato, a stack of thyme, 1/2 red onion, 3 green onions (cut in half), 1 cup of water.

step 4

Add the mixture from the blender onto the oxtails in a bowl. Massage seasonings into the oxtails. Let marinate overnight.

step 5

Take the oxtail from the fridge about 20 minutes before cooking to lower the temperature.

step 6

In a skillet, add olive oil to cover the bottom of the pan. Add the oxtails to sear each side.

step 7

Take the oxtail from the skillet and add to a dutch oven, add water to cover.

step 8

Because the meat is tough, continue to add water until the meat starts to fall away from the bone.

step 9

Skim the fat off of the top and disgard it.

step 10

Add sliced Red onions and about 1/2 cup green onions (chopped) and a green hot pepper. Reseason the oxtails again with powdered seasonings and fish meat sauce. Add a little ketchup and fresh thyme.

step 11

When the sauce has thickened, add the beans. Let cook until beans are tender.

step 12

Serve

ingredients

2 lbs of oxtail

1 tablespoon of Maggi all purpose seasoning

1 teaspoon of garlic powder

1 teaspoon of onion powder

1 teaspoon of black pepper

1 teaspoon paprika

½ teaspoon white pepper

2 cloves of garlic (minced)

1 tablespoon gravy master

1 tablespoon olive oil

½ cup frozen Lima Beans

1 plum tomato

½ of a medium onion

2 scallions

4-5 Fresh thyme sprigs

½ scotch bonnet pepper

3 tablespoon ketchup

1 tablespoon fish and meat sauce

1 bullion cube

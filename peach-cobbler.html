peach cobbler

No pie crust needed

prep time

15 mins

cook time

35 mins

amount of servings

1 cobbler

Tags : pie

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees

step 2

To make the filling: Peel the peaches, cut them in half lengthwise, and remove the pit, then slice the flesh into 3-4 wedges (as you would if you were making lemon wedges). In a large bowl, combine the peaches, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla. Transfer the peach mixture to a 2-quart baking dish and set aside.

step 3

To make the topping: In another bowl, combine the flower, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Then blend in the melted butter and slowly stir in the milk.

step 4

Pour the batter over the peache and sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar.

step 5

Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the peaches are tender and the crust is golden brown.

step 6

Serve the cobbler hot or at room temperature, by itself or with vanilla ice cream or creme fraiche.

ingredients

For the filling:
10 fresh, ripe peaches

1 cup light brown sugar

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

2 teaspoons cornstarch

2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

¼ teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest

½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

For the topping:
2 cups self-rising flour

1 cup grangulated sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon baking soda

½ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

¾ cups melted unsalted butter

1 cup whole milk (at room temperature)

½ teaspoon cinnamon sugar

peanut butter cookies

Adding peanut butter to cookies creates an amazing soft consistency with a very delicate, velvety crumb.

prep time

5 mins

cook time

10 mins

amount of servings

36 cookies

Tags : cookies

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees

step 2

Sift flour and baking powder together then whisk to combine.

step 3

Cream butter and sugars in a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment. You can add an optional 1/4-1/2 tsp sea salt.

step 4

Add peanut butter and mix until incorporated.

step 5

Mix in egg and vanilla extract then add flour mixture and beat until incorporated.

step 6

Mix in egg and vanilla extract then add flour mixture and beat until incorporated.

step 7

Flatten cookies with a fork in a criss-cross pattern.

step 8

Bake cookies for about 10 minutes.

step 9

Allow cookies to cool completely on baking sheet, they need to set up before being transferred.

ingredients

1½ cup all-purpose flour 180g

½ cup butter unsalted, room temp 113g

1 cup peanut butter 250g

½ cup brown sugar 100g, lightly packed

½ cup sugar 100g

1 tsp vanilla extract 5mL

egg large, room temp

¾ tsp baking powder 3g

For the topping:
2 cups self-rising flour

1 cup grangulated sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon baking soda

½ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

¾ cups melted unsalted butter

1 cup whole milk (at room temperature)

½ teaspoon cinnamon sugar

tools

Baking sheet

Stand mixer

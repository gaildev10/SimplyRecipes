Penne Alla Vodka

An Italian-American pasta dish of penne in a creamy, reddish-pink vodka sauce.

prep time

0 mins

cook time

0 mins

amount of servings

0 servings

Tags : pasta

instructions

step 1

In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add shallot and garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until softened (4-5 min.)

step 2

Add tomato paste and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring frequently, until paste has coated shallots and garlic and is beginning to darken. (about 5 min.)

step 3

Add vodka to pot and stir. (scraping up any browned bits). Turn off Heat.

step 4

Boil water with salt...cook pasta al dente.
Reserve 2 cups of pasta water.

step 5

Return sauce to medium heat and add ¼ cup of pasta water and heavy cream. Stir to combine. Add ½ the parmesan. Stir until melted. Turn off heat...add pasta. Add rest of parmesan, add pasta water (if needed)
Add basil

ingredients

3 tbsp butter

1 shallot, minced

2 cloves garlic, minced

½ cup tomato paste

½ tsp crushed red pepper flakes

2 tbsp vodka

kosher salt

1 lb tubed pasta (penne or rigatoni)

½ cup heavy cream

½ cup freshly grated parmesan

basil (for serving)

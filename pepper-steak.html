pepper steak

prep time

15 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : asian Tags : steak

instructions

step 1

Season beef. Mix well. Set aside.

step 2

Combine ingredients for the sauce in a bowl. Set aside.

step 3

Add ⅓ cup of oil to a wok. Brown beef in batches (Don't over crowd). Do not over cook beef (about 3 minutes). Remove from wok and set aside.

step 4

Drain most of the oil. Add a few drops of sesame oit into wok. Add peppers, ginger, onions and garlic. Stir for about 5 min. (on medium to low heat). Add black pepper.

step 5

Add sauce. Stir
(sauce will thicken...add water if sauce is too thick).

step 5

Add steak...and serve!

ingredients

Seasonings for Steak:
1 lb sirloin tip steak Thinly sliced

2 tbsp light soy sauce

2 tbsp dark soy sauce

1 egg white

1 tbsp mushroom seasoning ( optional)

½ tsp white pepper

2 tbsp cornstarch

Sauce:
2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp Hoisin sauce

2 tbsp oyster sauce

1 tbsp cornstarch mixed in 1/3 cup water

2 tbsp brown sugar

Veggies:
Chopped bell peppers as much as you like

Chopped onions as much as you like

1½ tbsp minced garlic

1½ tbsp grated ginger

½ tsp black pepper

tools

Wok

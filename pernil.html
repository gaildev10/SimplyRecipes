pernil

prep time

30 mins

cook time

4 hours

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, grate 4 cloves of garlic. Add a handful of garlic cloves. Add 1 cup of sofrito. Add the juice of 6 limes. Add 2 tbsp of salt, 2 tbsp of oregano, 1/4 cup soy sauce and mix.

step 2

poke the garlic cloves inside of the pork. Add the sauce all around the pernil.

step 3

Place the pernil inside of a Roasting Bag. (skin side up). Marinate for 3 days.

step 4

On roasting day, poke 3-4 holes at the top

step 5

Roasting time : 1 hour at 400 degrees, then 2.5 hours at 250 degrees.

step 6

Cut the pernil out of the bag. Then roast for 30 minutes

step 7

Shred the meat and cover it with the drippings. Cut the skin into small pieces.

step 8

Garnish with lime slices and serve.

philly cheesesteak sliders

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F

step 2

Season steak with salt and pepper to your liking

step 3

Heat a frying pan with a dash of olive oil

step 4

Add steak into the pan

step 5

Sear the steak until the outside is browned while also keeping the middle a tender pink color

step 6

Place the bottom of slider buns in a 9x13 baking pan

step 7

Place the steak aside, saute the pepper and onions in pan until they are tender

step 8

Prep the bread by spreading a layer of mayonnaise on the tops and bottoms of the slider buns

step 9

Layer the steak, peppers and onions, and provolone cheese on the bottom bun

step 10

Place the top slider buns over the layer of cheese and then brush the tops with melted butter and finally diced onions

step 11

Cover with foil and bake for 10 minutes, then remove foil and continue baking for another 10 minutes (or until cheese is melted)

ingredients

1 lb. Thinly sliced steak (a tender cut of beef, like a ribeye or sirloin)

1 Green pepper, diced

1 Onion, diced

6 slices Provolone cheese

3 tablespoons Butter, melted

1 package KING'S HAWAIIAN Original Hawaiian Sweet Slider Buns

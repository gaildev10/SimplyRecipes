pork egg-rolls

prep time

30 mins

cook time

4 hours

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : pork Tags : asian

instructions

step 1

Add oil to pan (just enough to coat the bottom of the pan)

step 2

Add onions

step 3

Add cabbage

step 4

Add bean sprouts

step 5

Season simply with kosher salt and pepper

step 6

Add a little soy sauce, garlic powder, onion powder , add fresh garlic and braised pork

step 7

Add filling to egg roll wrapper. seal edges with with water

step 8

Fry egg rolls

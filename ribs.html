Ribs

This simple recipe results in fluffy, tender pancakes with minimal effort

prep time

15 mins

cook time

3 hours

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

Pre-heat oven to 300° F

step 2

Rub ribs with lemon juice and rub

step 3

Bake for 2.5 hours

step 4

Brush on BBQ sauce

step 5

Bake another 5 min

ingredients

Dry Rub:
(Double the recipe)

¼ cup brown sugar

2 teaspoons chili powder

2 teaspoons paprika

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon dry mustard

1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves

½ teaspoon garlic powder

½ teaspoon onion powder

¼ teaspoon ground pepper

Prepare the Ribs:
1 rack of pork ribs

juice of 1 lemon

¼ cup of dry rub

½ cup barbecue sauce

roasted zucchini and mixed vegetables

A side dish that has a great combination of flavors

prep time

15 mins

cook time

1 hour

amount of servings

6 servings

Tags : vegetables

instructions

step 1

Chop vegetables

step 2

Add vegetables to wok with olive oil

step 3

Season with parsley and lemon juice

step 4

Bake at 350 F

ingredients

Yellow or Green Squash

1 green pepper or red pepper

1 pack cherry Tomatoes

1 onion

3 tbsp garlic powder

½ cup parsley

½ lemon juiced

roasted vegetables

prep time

10 mins

cook time

40 mins

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : vegetables

instructions

step 1

Pre-heat oven:
Set your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.

step 2

Prepare the Veggies:
Wash and dry the potatoes, carrots, and green beans. Halve the potatoes and trim the green beans.

step 3

Season the Veggies:
In a large bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Toss the potatoes and carrots in the mixture first.

step 4

Roast in the Oven:
Spread the potatoes and carrots evenly on the baking sheet. Roast for 20 minutes, then add the green beans and stir everything together. Roast for another 15-20 minutes until golden and crispy.

step 5

Garnish and Serve:
Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh parsley, and serve warm.

ingredients

1 lb baby potatoes, halved

½ lb baby carrots

½ lb green beans, trimmed

3 tbsp olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp dried thyme

1 tsp dried rosemary

Salt and pepper, to taste

2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

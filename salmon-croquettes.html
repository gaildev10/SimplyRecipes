salmon croquettes

prep time

15 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

4 patties

Tags : fish

instructions

step 1

Remove the bones from salmon.

step 2

Chop (or use a blender to chop garlic, onion peppers, parsley, celery, green onion and jalepeno).

step 3

In a bowl add the salmon, chopped veggies and egg.

step 4

To the bowl add a little flour, Tony's, paprika, old bay, onion powder, garlic powder and bread crumbs.

step 5

Form the mixture in the bowl into patties

step 6

Add oil to a skillet. Heat oil.

step 5

Cook patties about 3 mins on each side.

ingredients

1 can of pink salmon

1 garlic

1 red pepper

1 orange pepper

1 yellow pepper

1 jalepeno

green onions

fresh parsley

1 red onion

1 egg

2 tbsn flour

paprika

tony's cajun seasoning

old bay

onion powder

garlic powder

bread crumbs

oil

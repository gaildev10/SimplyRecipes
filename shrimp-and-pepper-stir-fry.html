shrimp and peppers stir fry

prep time

10 mins

cook time

15 mins

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : shrimp

instructions

step 1

Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.

step 2

Add shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque. Remove and set aside.

step 3

In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil. Stir-fry the garlic and ginger for 30 seconds until fragrant.

step 4

Toss in the red, yellow, and green bell peppers. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.

step 5

In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sesame oil, and chili flakes. Pour the mixture over the peppers, stirring well.

step 6

Return the shrimp to the skillet, and toss to combine. Cook for another 2 minutes until heated through.

step 6

Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds. Serve immediately with steamed rice or noodles.

ingredients

1 lb (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined

1 red bell pepper, sliced

1 yellow bell pepper, sliced

green bell pepper, sliced

2 tablespoons olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1-inch piece of ginger, minced

Sauce:
3 tablespoons soy sauce

2 tablespoons oyster sauce

tablespoon honey

1 teaspoon sesame oil

1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Garnish:
2 green onions, sliced

Sesame seeds for garnish

shrimp egg-rolls

prep time

15 mins

cook time

30 min

Amount of servings

Tags : shrimp Tags : asian

instructions

step 1

Prepare the shrimp:
chop shrimp then add to a bowl

step 2

Season shrimp with garlic powder
2 drops of sesame seed oil
ground ginger (3 sprinkles)
black pepper, salt and parsley.

step 3

In a wok, add a dab of butter + 2 tablespoons oil.
Once oil is hot, Add shrimp. Cook until no longer pink (don't overcook the shrimp).

step 4

Remove shrimp from pan and set aside.

step 5

Preparing the cole slaw:
Add 1 garlic (minced) to the hot butter/oil wok.

step 6

When garlic has cooked (about 2 mins)
add coleslaw mix
season with salt, black pepper, ginger and garlic.

step 9

Add shrimp and turn off wok

step 10

Add mixture to egg roll wraps

step 11

Fry Egg Rolls (about 5 mins)

ingredients

1 bag of raw peeled deveined medium-sized shrimps

coleslaw mix

oil

butter

ground ginger

garlic powder

sesame oil

salt

black pepper

parsley

garlic cloves

egg roll wrappers

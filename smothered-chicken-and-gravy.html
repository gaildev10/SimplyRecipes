smothered chicken & gravy

prep time

30 mins

cook time

30 mins

Amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Season chicken in a bowl with kosher salt, sazon, cajun seasoning, herb de provence, hot paprika, onion powder, dried fennel, lots of black pepper and garlic powder. Add buttermilk.

step 2

Massage the chicken and seasoning

step 3

In a another bowl, add flour. Coat each chicken piece with flour.

step 4

Add chicken to pre-heated oil pan (350 degrees).
Do not cook chicken fully, just half way. It will finish cooking in the gravy.

step 5

Preparing the Gravy

step 6

Remove most of the oil from frying the chicken

step 7

Add to the hot oil:
Saute celery, onions, and bell peppers

step 8

Add a couple of tablespoons of flour. Stir until roue gets brown.

step 9

Add hot water and continue to stir.

step 10

Add chicken boullion, garlic powder and black pepper

step 11

Add half fried chicken into the gravy pan and let it cook.

ingredients

To season the chicken:
chicken

kosher salt

black pepper

sazon

cajun seasoning

herb de provence (or italian seasoning)

hot paprika

onion powder

dried fennel

garlic powder

buttermilk

oil

all-purpose flour

Preparing the Gravy:
Onions

Bell pepper

Celery

2 tbsp all-purpose flour

chicken boullion

garlic powder

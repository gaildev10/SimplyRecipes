Stir Fry Vegetables with Chicken

A quick one-pan dish ready in 30 minutes

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

4 servings

Tags : vegetables

instructions

step 1

Cut chicken into bites.

step 2

Season chicken. Massage well. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes or longer.

step 3

Blanch the carrots and broccoli in a pot of water (about 10 minutes)

step 4

Transfer the carrots and broccoli into a bowl with cold water.

step 5

Mix the sauce and set aside.

step 6

In a wok, cover the bottom with oil.

step 7

In a heated wok, add the chicken. (cook each side about 5 min.)
Set chicken to the side.

step 8

In a heated wok, add garlic and ginger. Stir about 2 min.

step 9

Add chili pepper flakes. Stir.

step 9

Add peppers and onions ( fry them about 2 mins on high heat.)

step 10

Add broccoli and carrots

step 11

Add sauce

step 12

Add chicken

step 12

Enjoy with rice.

ingredients

Boneless Chicken Thighs

1 each Red, Yellow, Green Bell Peppers

2 carrots

1 broccoli head

1 tbsp ginger (minced)

1 tbsp garlic (minced)

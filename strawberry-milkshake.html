strawberry milkshake

prep time

10 mins

cook time

10 mins

amount of servings

3 servings

Tags : drinks

instructions

step 1

Add strawberries, milk, cream, jam and ice cream to a high speed blender. Cover with lid.

step 2

Begin blending on low speed and gradually increase to high speed. Blend until smooth.

step 3

Pour into 3 glasses and serve immediately, or pour mixture into popsicles moulds (inserting wooden sticks) and freeze for 6 hours.

ingredients

1 lb. strawberries, chilled, stems trimmed

½ cup milk

¼ cup heavy cream

3 Tbsp strawberry jam (preferably freezer jam)

10 oz. (3 cups) strawberry ice cream or vanilla ice cream (such as Tillamook)

Sweetened whipped cream, for topping (optional)

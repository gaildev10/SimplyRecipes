stuffed peppers soup

prep time

5 mins

cook time

30 mins

amount of servings

Tags : soups

instructions

step 1

Heat skillet with a little oil. Brown the beef and sauage.

step 2

Add chopped onion and garlic.

step 3

Once the meet is browned, add peppers, diced tomatoes and crushed tomatoes, seasonings, rice and beef stock. Stir together.

step 4

Cook until the peppers are softened. Add parsley.

ingredients

1 lb ground beef

1 lb italian sausage

1 red pepper

1 green pepper

1 onion

2 garlic cloves

beef stock

orangano

italian seasonings

diced tomatoes

crushed tomatoes

cooked rice

fresh parsley (garnish)

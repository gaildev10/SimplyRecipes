Sweet Potato Pie

Top rated Sweet Potato Pie recipe

prep time

15 mins

cook time

45 mins

amount of servings

1 pie

Tags : pies Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Heat oven to 425.

step 2

Prepare Pastry

step 3

Combine sugar, flour, baking powder and nutmeg in medium mixer bowl. Add remaining ingredients and beat until well blended.

step 4

Place pastry-lined pie plate on oven rack; pour in filling. Bake 15 minutes.
Reduce oven temperature to 350. Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean, about 45 minutes longer.

ingredients

2 cups hot cooked mashed sweet potatoes
(about 2 medium sweet potatoes)

¾ cups sugar

1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

½ teaspoon baking powder

½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

½ teaspoon cinnamon

¼ teaspoon all-spice

¼ cup butter

1 egg, slightly beaten

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1¼ cups evaporated milk

tools

Pastry for 9-inch one-crust pie

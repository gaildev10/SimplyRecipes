Tacos

prep time

20 mins

cook time

5.5 hours

oven temperature

350

Tags : beef

instructions

step 1

Cut chicken into bite sizes. (cubed)

step 2

Season chicken with taco seasoning.

step 3

Cut up peppers, onion, garlic, tomatillos (husk removed).

step 4

Roast veggies on a griddle

step 4

Add veggies to a blender...add parsley, salt (a touch), and lime juice (a touch).
After blending...set aside

step 5

Add chicken to Griddle on high heat. Once chicken is nice and crispy, warm tortilla on top.

step 6

Remove chicken and continue browning tortillas.

step 7

To the tortillas add chicken, a little onion bits, tomato bits, add salsa and garnish with parsley.

tea cakes

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20 mins

amount of servings

1 dozen cookies

Tags : cookies Tags : desserts

instructions

step 1

Blend in mixer butter and sugar.

step 2

Add eggs.

step 3

In another bowl add flour, salt and baking soda and nutmeg.

step 4

Portion the flour into the mixing bowl ( Add in thirds).

step 5

Add lemon and vanilla extract.

step 6

Add dough to a floured surface.

step 7

Add as much flour as needed to no longer be sticky.

step 8

Mold dough into a rectangle, wrap in cellophane. Place in refrigerator for 3 hours.

step 9

Warm dough with hands. Begin rolling dough with rolling pin.

step 10

Use cookie cutter to make the cookies.

step 10

Place in oven at 325 for 20 minutes.

ingredients

1 cup butter

1¾ cup sugar

2 large eggs

3½ cups flour

½ teaspoon baking soda

¼ teaspoon nutmeg

1 tsp vanilla extract

½ tsp lemon extract

tools

Cookie cutter

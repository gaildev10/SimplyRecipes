baked turkey wings

prep time

20 min

cook time

3hours 45min

amount of servings

Tags : turkey

instructions

step 1

Pre-heat oven to 350

step 2

Wash wings in cold water - Pat dry

step 3

Season wings and massage

step 4

Place seasoned wings in a baking dish

step 5

Add vinegar (around wing, not on them)

step 6

Add water (around wing, not on them)

step 7

Cover and bake for 3 hours

step 8

Remove cover and bake another 45 min

Roasted Turkey

A great Holiday recipe

prep time

25 mins

cook time

5.5 hours

oven temperature

350

Tags : turkey

instructions

step 1

over softened butter, add minced garlic, fresh thyme and rosemary. Mix well. Set aside.

step 2

Rub:
Mix together seasonings.

step 3

Season the Turkey:
To a room-temperatured, dry turkey, drizzle 3 tablespoon of olive oil all over the turkey.

step 4

Add butter/garlic inside& outside skin of turkey.

step 4

Add rub onto turkey.

step 5

spinkle fresh thyme, rosemany, sage on top of turkey.

step 6

Cover cheesecloth in butter; cover turkey with cloth

step 7

Bake turkey in 350 degree oven for about 5-5.5 hours

ingredients

2 tablespoon onion powder

1 tablespoon garlic powder

1 tablespoon adobo seasoning
